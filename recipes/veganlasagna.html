Vegan Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients - traditionally, meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles. However, this recipe is for all you vegan food lovers out there, follow this recipe and enjoy the finished product!
